Beginer's guide


WASD Key

This is the basic key of movements:

  • W: Up (Middle finger)

  • S: Down (Middle finger)

  • A: Left (Ring finger)

  • D: Right (Index finger)


Mouse

The basic control for most computers

In gaming,mouse plays an important role in navigating angle view in FPS and position in MOBA, execute specific actions.


Other keys:

  • Space(Thumb finger): Jump

  • Shift(Pinky fibger): Crouch

  • Number keys:Mainly for changing items
